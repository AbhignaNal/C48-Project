Interesting Facts About Butterflies!

  • Butterflies get nutrients form drinking from mud puddles.
  • They can only fly at temperatures under 55 degrees farenheit.
  • They have eyes that are able to ultraviolet rays and so they can see many colors the human eye can't.

Interesting Facts About Cranes!

  • Some cranes have the ability to fly over the Himalayas, the tallest mountain range in the world.
  • They have a mating dance that is fun to watch.
  • They live in almost all the continents excluding South America and Antartica.
